About


Hello all! My name is Corinna, and I am the creator of Kids Glam Studio in San Dimas, CA! As a mother of two beautiful daughters, I understand how important it is to create a foundation of confidence for young girls that they can carry with them as they grow up. That's why I have created Kids Glam Studio, a place where girls can realize their inner and outer beauty. My Glam Squad ensures every girl has a safe place to find her inner diva in hopes that she will continue to shine everywhere she goes once she walks out our doors. Trust us to make your little divas' next event as glamorous as they are!
